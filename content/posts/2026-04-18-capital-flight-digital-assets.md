As geopolitical instability intensifies, investors are accelerating their move toward safe-haven assets. Along with gold, digital assets like Bitcoin are gaining significant attention, with a particularly noticeable increase in the participation of institutional capital. The recent trend of increased purchases of Bitcoin ETFs (Exchange Traded Funds) by institutional investors clearly demonstrates this movement.

This capital shift goes beyond mere asset diversification; it reflects a shift in the perception of the reliability and stability of digital assets. As uncertainty regarding the existing financial system grows, digital assets based on blockchain technology are being recognized as safer havens. However, this change simultaneously highlights the need for regulation in the digital asset market, along with the potential risks that follow.

Ultimately, if geopolitical risks persist, the movement of capital into digital assets is likely to accelerate further, which may serve as a signal foretelling structural changes in the global financial market.
