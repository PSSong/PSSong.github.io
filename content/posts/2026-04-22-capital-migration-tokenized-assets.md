Recent moves by global financial institutions, particularly BlackRock and JP Morgan, suggest that capital is rapidly shifting from traditional financial markets to programmable digital assets. The growth of not only cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum but also tokenized assets reflects a new trend of capital seeking to overcome the limitations of the existing financial system.

This change is based on the transparency, efficiency, and decentralized nature of digital assets, foreshadowing structural changes in the future financial system. Through these trends, corporations are exploring new investment opportunities and risk management strategies, which is interpreted as a significant signal reshaping the future of the financial markets.
