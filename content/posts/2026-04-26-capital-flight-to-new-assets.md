April 26, 2026—In global capital markets, a distinct shift of capital from bonds toward digital assets and AI-based finance is becoming evident. This change reflects a fundamental transformation in the economic structure, demonstrating rising investment interest in new asset classes away from traditional financial systems.

The approval of Bitcoin ETFs and the proliferation of financial services utilizing AI technology are accelerating this trend. Investors are moving away from traditional fixed-income assets and turning their attention to digital assets and AI finance, which offer higher returns and innovative investment opportunities. This movement of capital heralds not only innovation in financial markets but also structural changes across the entire economy.

Ultimately, the shift of capital toward new asset classes reflects confidence in the technologies and innovations that will drive the future of the economy, signaling a fundamental restructuring of the financial system.
