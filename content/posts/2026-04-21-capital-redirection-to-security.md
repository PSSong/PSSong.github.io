Recent capital flows are undergoing a rapid restructuring, moving away from a focus on economic growth toward assets that emphasize security and scarcity. Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology, digital assets such as Bitcoin, and energy resources are positioned at the core of this trend. This change reflects a transition in the global economy toward a new paradigm that places greater value on security and stability.

AI is being recognized as an essential element for securing future economic security and technological superiority, leading to a surge in investment in related companies and technologies. Among digital assets, Bitcoin is drawing attention as a hedge for economic security due to its scarcity and decentralized characteristics. Energy resources are also becoming a primary target for capital, as the importance of a stable energy supply is emphasized amidst geopolitical instability.

This reallocation of capital reflects strategic decision-making for the long-term survival and security of nations and corporations, going beyond the mere pursuit of economic profit. As uncertainty in the global economic environment increases, the movement toward assets that prioritize security and scarcity is expected to accelerate further.
