Recent changes in the relationship between Microsoft and OpenAI are being interpreted as a significant signal reshaping the landscape of the technology industry. Along with this change, the phenomenon of capital flows moving toward Amazon suggests that the industry's focus is shifting from the innovative development of AI technology toward building more stable infrastructure and expanding market share.

Amazon already holds a strong position in the field of cloud computing, and based on this, it is focusing more on the utilization and expansion of AI technology. This movement of capital reflects a change in the strategic approach to viewing the future of the tech industry, going beyond simple competition between companies. Rather than innovative technology development, priority is being given to improving efficiency through existing infrastructure and strengthening competitiveness within the market.

This restructuring can be seen as an important example showing how the advancement of AI technology goes beyond mere technical innovation to integrate into corporate business models and market strategies. Future trends are expected to further increase interest in how tech companies will harmoniously pursue stable growth strategies alongside innovation.
