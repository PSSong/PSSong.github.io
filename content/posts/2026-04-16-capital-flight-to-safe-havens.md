As geopolitical instability and uncertainty in financial markets increase, capital is undergoing a massive migration from traditional risky assets to safe-haven assets. In particular, the shift toward institutional safe-haven assets is prominent, manifesting as increased investment in Bitcoin ETFs and large financial institutions. Institutions such as BlackRock and JPMorgan are drawing attention as stable investment destinations within this trend.

This movement of capital reflects the tendency of investors to seek stability amidst uncertainty. In the case of Bitcoin ETFs, despite the volatility of the cryptocurrency market, they are attracting investor interest based on trust in regulation and stability within the institutional framework. Meanwhile, traditional financial institutions are still recognized as reliable safe-haven assets and are positioned advantageously to attract capital during periods of market instability.

However, the impact of this great migration on long-term investment strategies must be considered. Investors should keep in mind that the pursuit of short-term stability may result in missing long-term growth opportunities.
