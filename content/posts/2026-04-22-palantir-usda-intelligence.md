The contract Palantir has signed with the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) holds significance beyond strengthening food security. This contract can be interpreted as a strategic move to digitalize and control the nation's core infrastructure through the collection and analysis of agricultural data. Palantir's advanced data analytics technology, alongside improving agricultural productivity, provides the government with the ability to monitor and intervene in all aspects of the agricultural sector in real-time.

This approach does not merely stop at strengthening food security; it reflects a dual-purpose strategy to enhance social control through data at a national level. While the integrated management of agricultural data can increase transparency and efficiency in future decision-making processes, it also carries the potential to threaten the data privacy and autonomy of individuals and corporations. Therefore, this contract is evaluated as a significant step toward expanding the state's data control capabilities under the guise of food security.
