While the tech market is showing overall weakness, capital flows are concentrating on core semiconductor companies such as Intel and TSMC. This reflects a polarization phenomenon within the tech sector and demonstrates that investors are reaffirming their confidence in companies with future growth engines.

Despite volatility in the Nasdaq, the semiconductor industry stands at the center of continuous demand and technological innovation, being recognized as a safe haven for capital. In particular, the advancement of next-generation technologies such as artificial intelligence, 5G, and cloud computing is further highlighting the growth potential of semiconductor companies. This concentration of capital is a process of re-evaluating competitiveness within the tech sector and is expected to serve as a crucial indicator for predicting future market trends.
