## What We Cover

**Global Pulse** reads between the lines of global affairs — not what happened, but what it means and what isn't being said.

- **Geopolitics** — Great power dynamics, regional conflicts, diplomatic signals
- **Economy** — Macro trends, monetary policy, structural shifts in trade
- **Tech & Policy** — Technology regulation, AI policy, data sovereignty
- **Market Signals** — What markets are saying and what they aren't
- **Weekly Digest** — The week's essential developments, distilled

## Why This Exists

News is abundant; context is scarce. Headlines tell you what happened. We focus on the timing, the omissions, and the connections — the space between the lines where the real story often lives.

No political sides. No conspiracy theories. Just publicly available facts, pattern recognition, and careful inference.

Updated regularly. Stay tuned.
