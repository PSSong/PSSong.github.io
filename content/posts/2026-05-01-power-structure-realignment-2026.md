Issues continuing since April 30, 2026, have demonstrated the expanding influence of the Trump family and tech giants. This time, with the combination of the Trump family's crypto enterprise and Elon Musk's political influence, the emergence of a new private power structure—fusing technology, capital, and politics—is being observed.

The Trump family's crypto enterprise is presenting a new approach to the financial system through innovation in digital assets. This movement is creating new capital flows that transcend traditional financial institutions, serving as a foundation for the expansion of political influence. In particular, the Trump family's network is making international capital movement more efficient by utilizing cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.

Meanwhile, while Elon Musk has already established himself as a leader in technological innovation through Tesla and SpaceX, he has recently significantly expanded his political influence as well. Musk's statements and actions garner global attention, and his opinions exert considerable influence not only on technology policy but also on international politics. In particular, since his acquisition of Twitter, his ability to disseminate information and shape public opinion through social media platforms has been further strengthened.

As these two elements combine, a new private power structure is being formed, where technological innovation, capital power, and political influence merge into one. This structure represents a new form of power that transcends existing state-led power structures, and its potential impact on international politics and economics is immense.

However, there are also points of caution behind these changes. Concerns are being raised that their convergence could disrupt the balance between nations, and that if the influence of specific individuals or corporations expands excessively, it could threaten democracy and a fair competitive environment. Therefore, continuous monitoring and a balanced approach to the development and impact of this new power structure are necessary.

The emergence of this new power structure demonstrates the complex interaction between technological innovation, capital, and politics, foreshadowing significant changes in the future global political and economic landscape.
