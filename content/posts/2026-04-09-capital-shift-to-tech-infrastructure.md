Despite geopolitical instability, global capital is accelerating its shift toward technological infrastructure, particularly Artificial Intelligence (AI) data centers and digital assets. Investments in digital assets, including Bitcoin ETFs, are also noticeably increasing.

**Reading Between the Lines**: This movement of capital goes beyond a simple investment trend; it reflects a tendency to recognize technological infrastructure as a key driver of future economic growth. As geopolitical risks rise, the phenomenon of funds flocking to stable and high-growth sectors demonstrates that technological innovation and digital assets are being regarded as central to long-term value creation.

Institutional investors are placing their trust in the stability and growth potential provided by AI and blockchain technologies, even amidst an uncertain geopolitical environment. This trend suggests that technological infrastructure is establishing itself as a core pillar of the future economy.
